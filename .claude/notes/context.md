# Context

## Tesa Company Overview

Tesa is a global adhesive solutions manufacturer with 125+ years of history, operating as part of the Beiersdorf Group. Based in Hamburg, Germany, they specialize in adhesive tapes and self-adhesive solutions across two main segments:

- **Consumer Products**: Bathroom accessories, insect screens, mounting solutions, repair tapes, office supplies
- **Industrial Solutions**: Bonding, sealing, and adhesive applications for automotive, electronics, appliances, solar energy, and other industries

Website: [www.tesa.com](https://www.tesa.com/en)

## Business Unit: General Industry

One of Tesa's main business divisions, focused on industrial adhesive solutions:

- **Core Focus**: Develops and produces adhesive tapes for a wide range of industrial applications
- **Key Industries Served**: Automotive, electronics, renewable energy, and manufacturing
- **Main Applications**:
  - Bonding
  - Masking
  - Surface protection
  - Sealing
- **Approach**: Delivers customized adhesive solutions for manufacturing and industrial processes
